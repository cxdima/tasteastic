Tasteastic makes it easy to track your nutrients

Nutrition is about eating a healthy and balanced diet. Food and drink provide the energy and nutrients you need to be healthy.

Amino Acids

Amino acids are the building blocks of proteins. The body produces many amino acids and others come from food.

Glucose

Glucose — also called blood sugar — is the main sugar found in the blood and the main source of energy for your body.

Calories

A unit of energy in food. Carbohydrates, fats, protein, and alcohol in the foods and drinks we eat provide food energy or "calories."

Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates are one of the main types of nutrients. It stores any extra sugar in your liver and muscles for when it is needed.

Cholesterol

Your body needs some cholesterol to make hormones, vitamin D, and substances that help you digest foods. Cholesterol is also found in some of the foods you eat. High levels of cholesterol in the blood can increase your risk of heart disease.

Gluten

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, rye, and barley. It can also be in products such as vitamin and nutrient supplements, lip balms, and certain medicines.
